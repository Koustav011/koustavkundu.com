About

I started on the operations floor — nearly three years at TCS running retail-banking and custody processes, where I learned how banks actually move money: due diligence, maker–checker, MIS, and the thousand small controls behind every transaction.

An MBA in Finance later, I moved to the product side. At ICICI Bank's Business Loans Group I own the renewals portfolio and the machinery around it — building the portfolio waterfall, then working with DSAG and Policy to automate it end-to-end (yes, including UAT on the input files). I like the unglamorous middle of product work: getting policy, tech and business to agree on the same file format.

Off hours, my desk looks different — a Raspberry Pi home server, Python screeners for Indian equities, and small pipelines that stitch LLM APIs into daily workflows. The rule of the lab is simple: if a task repeats twice, it gets a script.
